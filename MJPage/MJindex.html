Here's a timeline of Michael Jordan's career:

  • 1963 - Born in Brooklyn, New York.
  • 1982 - Hits the game-winning shot in the NCAA championship.
  • 1984 - Drafted by the Chicago Bulls.
  • 1991 - Wins his first NBA championship with the Bulls.
  • 1996 - Returns to the NBA after first retirement and wins more titles.
  • 2003 - Retires for the final time.
